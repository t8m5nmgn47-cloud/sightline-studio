About

Big-company insight, built for the corner shop.

Large companies pay specialists thousands to tell them how they look from the outside. Local businesses get nothing — not because they matter less, but because no one made it simple or affordable. We’re fixing that.

Sightline started from a simple frustration: a great local business can be quietly losing customers — to a slow website, a wrong listing, an unanswered review, or a small security gap — and have no idea, because the tools that surface these things were built for IT teams, priced for corporations, and written in jargon.

So we built the opposite. One report, in plain English, that shows an owner exactly how their business looks from the outside — across website, security, and reputation — using only public information. A free scan to see where you stand, and an affordable monthly watch so you’re never the last to know.

We care a lot about doing this the right way. Everything we do is passive and public. We don’t hack, we don’t snoop on people, and we’re upfront about what we can and can’t see. That restraint isn’t a limitation — it’s the point. Read our ethics →
