New here?

Here's exactly what your first Sunday looks like.

No pressure, no spotlight. Walk in, grab a coffee, and stay as long as you like. Here's the plan so nothing's a surprise.

1

Easy parking

Free lot with reserved first-time spots near the main doors — look for the Welcome signs.

2

Walk right in

A friendly host will greet you and point you to coffee and a seat — that's it.

3

Kids are covered

Safe, secure check-in for newborn through 5th grade, with background-checked volunteers.

4

About an hour

Music, a down-to-earth message, and you're free to head out or hang around.

What we believe

A safe, welcoming place — in every way.

We believe church should feel like home. Here's what that looks like in practice.

✝

Grace, not performance

No dress code, no perfect life required. You're welcome exactly as you are — doubts, questions, and all.

🔒

Secure kids check-in & giving

Every child has a secure, background-verified check-in. Online giving is encrypted and handled by a trusted platform.

🤝

Genuinely inclusive

Real diversity of age, background, and story. We work hard to make sure no one feels invisible or out of place here.
