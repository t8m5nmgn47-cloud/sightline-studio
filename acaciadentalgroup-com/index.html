Are you accepting new patients?

Yes — we’re welcoming new patients and families this month, with same-week appointments often available. Booking takes about a minute online or by phone.

Do you take my insurance?

We work with most major plans and file the paperwork for you. No insurance? Ask about our simple in-house membership.

I get nervous at the dentist — can you help?

Absolutely. Gentle, judgment-free care is what we’re known for. We explain everything, move at your pace, and offer comfort options so visits feel easy.

What if I have an emergency?

Call us first. We hold time in the schedule every day for emergencies and will get you seen and comfortable as fast as possible.
