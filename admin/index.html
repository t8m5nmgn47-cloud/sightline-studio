Launch a new customer page
1

Add the prospect

Add the business (name, domain, city, vertical, rating) to top30.json. The generator pulls brand colors automatically; drop a hero photo at images/custom/<domain>.jpg for a hand-picked look.

2

Build & QA

Run ./release.sh — it regenerates the demo (8 styles), teardown, and funnel, then runs the QA gate. It stops if anything fails.

3

Deploy

Run ./deploy.sh "added <business>" — it re-checks QA and pushes live. The new funnel appears at /p/<slug>/ and in the pipeline. Send the link.

4

When they buy → add them as a subscriber & go live

Add them in Subscribers above, finalize content, switch on the add-ons they bought, point their domain at the site, turn on the secure portal (legal/medical), and install the legal pages. (Full steps in the Operations Playbook & Go-Live Runbook.)
